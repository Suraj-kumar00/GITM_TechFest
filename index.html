    • About GITM Tech Fest 2024

    Every year, GITM College organizes a tech fest to help students learn more about technology and come up with new ideas. The main goal of this event is to improve students' technical skills and encourage them to think creatively. It's a place where students can share their original ideas and solutions in a supportive environment.

    The Event Includes

    1. Prototype Model
    2. Debugging
    3. Coding
    4. E-sports
    5. Technical Rangoli
    6. Photography and Reel
    7. Paper Presentation
    8. Business Plan
    9. Ad Mad Show
